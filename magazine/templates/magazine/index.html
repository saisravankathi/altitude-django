About

  • Our Humble Beginnings

    Since inception in 2003, Altitudes magazine has cemented it’s position as the leading lifestyle publication dedicated to business aviation. With a global reach exceeding 340,000 individuals, Altitudes offers unique industry insight, information and in-depth articles for the growing group of high net worth individuals who own or charter business aircraft.

  • Business Aviation

    Considered the leading title in business aviation, our editorial content features in-depth interviews with captains of industry, exclusive reviews of all the latest jets from every manufacturer, design inspiration, technical analysis and updates on the latest developments in the private jet industry. We also carry unique lifestyle features of interest to our high net worth readership, including luxury travel features, high-end vehicle reviews, the latest watch reveals, art at auction and the superyachts making waves. Taking full advantage of our large format glossy spreads, our stunning features are rich in detail, design and photography.

  • Business News

    Featuring engaging content and regularly updated business aviation news, as well as the current and back issues of the magazines, the dynamic Altitudes site offers in-depth articles, aircraft listings, charter contacts, forthcoming events and luxury lifestyle features in an accessible and exciting format.
